I am a senior undergraduate student studying Computer Sciences and Psychology at University of Wisconsin-Madison. I conduct research in human-computer interaction (HCI), social computing, and human-AI interaction.

My research is driven by the questions of how to improve social technologies with richer and more contextualized representations of information and how to support co-augmentation between human and machine intelligence. I aim to design and build social computing and hybrid human-AI tools and systems to improve user experience and address real-world issues brought or exacerbated by computing technologies (e.g., algorithmic bias and misinformation).
